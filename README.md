# Temple Bridge Human Center

Temple Bridge Human Center is a mobile-first cultural guide for Maple Bridge, Hanshan Temple, Tieling Pass, and the Grand Canal. The site turns a visit into a clear walking experience, helping people understand where they are, what each place means, and how the landscape connects with poetry, history, and local life.

## Website Features

- Interactive entry page centered on Maple Bridge, with a clear first view and quick access to important nearby spots.
- Spot detail pages for Maple Bridge, Hanshan Temple, Tieling Pass, and the Grand Canal, each combining location, story, poem, and old-versus-new visual comparison.
- Three themed routes: Quick Visit, Poetry Route, and Grand Canal Route, designed for different visit lengths and interests.
- Route detail maps with ordered stops, visit progress, custom memory pins, and story challenges.
- Search panel for quickly opening a spot or route without browsing through every page.
- Cultural mini games, poetry questions, collectible stamps, and unlockable story cards.
- Personal journey summary with score, visited spots, unlocked stories, collected seals, favorite quote, and shareable memory.

## Website Advantages

- Easy to use outdoors on a phone, with large buttons, short text, and clear visual hierarchy.
- Makes cultural information easier to understand by connecting every story to a real place on the route.
- Balances practical navigation with a poetic Jiangnan atmosphere, so the site feels both useful and memorable.
- Encourages active exploration instead of passive reading through stamps, quizzes, route progress, and unlockable content.
- Works for first-time visitors who need guidance and local residents who want to rediscover familiar places.
- Uses a refined modern Chinese visual style inspired by bridges, canal water, ink, stone, and warm cinnabar accents.
